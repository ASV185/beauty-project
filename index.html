My Story


MIELLE was created by Monique Rodriguez, a registered nurse, wife and mother of two girls, who desired to share her healthy hair journey using products with organic ingredients to achieve amazing results. She knew the importance of knowing what is in your products and wanted to create healthier options for the whole family. The result is a brand that delivers to you natural and effective products.

- Story written in MIELLE Rosemary Mint Daily Styling Créme

Essential Oils & Hair Growth

Normally, essential oils in most adults help promote hair growth by reducing stress, have antibacterial properties, improve scalp health, improve cellular generation that helps with hair thickness and hair growth, to having antifungal properties, and most importantly, reducing dandruff that helps reduce hair loss.

Biotin

In many cases, biotin is able to prevent more hair loss and hair growth for people deficient in biotin. Biotin deficiency is one of many reasons for hair loss, and Mielle Rosemary Mint Daily Styling Créme contains biotin as one of its many ingredients.
